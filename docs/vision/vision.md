# CoSiMo Vision

> **One platform, every journey.**

---

## Purpose

CoSiMo exists to make connected mobility accessible to everyone — regardless of age, ability, language, or digital confidence. It acts as an AI-driven concierge that guides users through a mobility experience from start to finish, adapting to each person rather than asking them to adapt to the system.

The MonoCab is the primary and exemplary context for CoSiMo. Everything is designed and validated there first. But the architecture is built to transfer — to any environment where people need guidance through a connected, intelligent system.

---

## The Problem

Connected mobility environments are complex. Vehicles communicate with infrastructure. Journeys require booking, authentication, real-time feedback, and physical navigation. For many people, this complexity is a barrier. CoSiMo removes that barrier by placing a single, coherent interface between the user and everything the system does.

---

## Who It Is For

CoSiMo is for anyone interacting with a CoSiMo-enabled environment. There is no assumed default user. The system learns from a user's persona — their preferences, needs, and context — and shapes the interaction accordingly. The interface follows the person, not a template.

Groups with particular relevance include older people, children travelling alone, people with visual, hearing, cognitive, or physical impairments, and anyone in an unfamiliar or stressful mobility situation. These groups are not edge cases — designing for them produces a better system for everyone.

---

## Core Principles

**Persona-driven interaction**
Every user has a persona that defines how CoSiMo communicates with them — the modality, the pace, the language, the level of detail. No two interactions need to look the same.

**Inclusive by default**
Inclusivity is not a feature added on top. It is the starting point. Voice, touch, visual, and other interaction modes are supported because different people need different things — and the same person may need different things at different times.

**Privacy by design**
Privacy is not a compliance checkbox. It is a design principle applied from the first decision onwards. Data is used to serve the user in the moment, not to build persistent profiles or enable third-party access.

**Open by default**
CoSiMo is built on open standards. No part of the architecture should create dependency on a closed vendor ecosystem. This is a firm principle, not a preference.

**Technology in service of people**
AI and IoT are the broad enabling technologies. They are chosen because they serve the vision — not because they are the most sophisticated option available. Simplicity and reliability take precedence.

**Presence beyond the screen**
CoSiMo is not an app. Interaction is spatial, multisensory, and situational — light, sound, and physical environment are all part of how the system communicates. The concierge meets users where they are, not where a screen happens to be.

---

## What Success Looks Like

Within a 1–2 year horizon, success means two things delivered together:

1. **A working prototype** — CoSiMo demonstrated on the MonoCab, handling real interactions across different user personas.
2. **A reusable architecture** — a documented, transferable system design that another team or institution can adopt for a different context without starting from scratch.

---

## Beyond the MonoCab

The MonoCab is where CoSiMo is proven. But the vision is broader. Any environment that combines connected infrastructure with a need for accessible human guidance is a potential context — a museum, a transport hub, a campus. The architecture is designed so that the core platform stays the same and the context changes around it.

---

## The Character of the Concierge

CoSiMo is a companion, not a gatekeeper. It guides, supports, and translates — between the user and the system, between uncertainty and confidence, between complexity and clarity. It is present when needed and unobtrusive when not.

The concierge should feel like a trusted, always-available point of contact: calm, clear, and on the user's side. It carries emotional weight — helping people feel safe and oriented in unfamiliar or demanding situations.

---

## What CoSiMo Is Not

CoSiMo will never be a proprietary system. The moment the architecture becomes dependent on closed tools, locked APIs, or vendor-specific platforms, it fails its own purpose. Openness is non-negotiable.

CoSiMo is not a surveillance tool. It does not monitor users, build behavioural profiles, or act in the interest of any party other than the person it is serving. It is not intrusive — it does not initiate unnecessary interactions, does not push unsolicited information, and does not make itself more visible than the situation requires.
